Turquoise in Dreams

Turquoise in a dream is symbolic of healing power, karma and natural energy. It is often associated with the sun, fire, and strength.

Seeing turquoise color in a dream is a precursor of goodwill of fate, good luck in your personal life, success in solving important issues. There will be excellent business prospects, and constructive changes will begin soon.

Seeing clothes of turquoise color in a dream is a promise of good perspectives in business sphere. You will successfully negotiate, sign a good contract, start a profitable business with partners.

If you were wearing very bright shades of turquoise clothes in a dream, this symbol means you will have enough energy and sense of purpose in order to achieve your intended goal without any help.

This color in a dream also represents curing powers. So, if an ill person saw turquoise clothes in a dream, he will recover soon. If the healthy person saw such dream, this color is a sign of even stronger health.
