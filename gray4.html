Gray in Dreams

In dreams, the color gray indicates fear, fright, depression, ill health, ambivalence and confusion. You may feel emotionally distant, isolated, or detached. Alternatively, the color gray symbolizes your individualism.

Calm and cool shades of gray seen in a dream are explained by thoughtfulness, high spirituality of the dreamer.

To dream of a gray suit or dress means longing for the past times. Dreaming of trying on or buying gray clothes means that you have no strength left to fight and you are ready to surrender. Neutral gray suits, fur coats and dresses also predict stagnation of projects, unwillingness to promote affairs, and desire to live in peace.
