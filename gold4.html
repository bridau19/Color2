Gold in Dreams

In dreams, The golden color reflects a spiritual reward, richness, refinement and enhancement of your surroundings. It also signifies your determination and unyielding nature.

If in the dream you see a known person wearing a golden gress or wearing a dress made of gold, means that person is valuable and intelligent and that your love or your friendship is precious.

Clothes of golden color seen in a dream predict solving all your financial problems. If you see a brocade dress in a dream, then you can hope to get acquainted with a wealthy and generous partner who will take part in improving your well-being. But golden pants or skirt promise to win in a dispute or in court.
